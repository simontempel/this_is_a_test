The University of Vienna

This website serves as a short introduction to the University of Vienna and its history.

First a few facts about the University:

  • The University of Vienna has about 85.000 Students witch makes it the largest University in Austria.
  • The University of Vienna employs about 10.000 People for research and teaching.
  • The University of Vienna offers about 180 degree programms, ranging from humanities to natural sciences.

A short History of the University of Vienna:

  • The university was Founded in 1365 by Rudolf IV, making it the oldest university in the German speaking world.
  • Over the course of the next few hundred years the University grew into the biggest one in the Holy Roman Empire.
  • In 1623 Emperor Ferdinand II incorporated the Jesuit College into the university.
  • Under Empress maria Theresa the Jesuits lost their influence and the university went under state influence.
  • After the revolution 1848 the university achieved a large degree of freedom from the goverment.
  • In 1897 women were first admitted into the university, although this was limited to the subject of philosophy.
  • Under the Nazi Regime jewish students and teachers were expelled from the university.
